## E_TYPE Table

This is the flag which defines the intended linking behaviour of our executable.

|Code |Alias    |Description  |
|-----|---------|-------------|
|0    |ET_NONE  |Unknown      |
|1    |ET_REL   |Relocatable  |
|2    |ET_EXEC  |Executable   |
|3    |ET_DYN   |Shared Object|
|4    |ET_CORE  |Core         |
